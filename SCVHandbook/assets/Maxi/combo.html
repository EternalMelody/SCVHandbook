The Comprehensive Maxi Combo List!


This list should be pretty much complete, minus B+K combos (I can hardly ever get that move off, making testing combos a bitch) and Wall combos, which may be added later if people contribute them.

Barring any strange hitbox issues, these combos should all be COMPLETELY guaranteed (meaning no teching, blocking, or air control can escape) with one exception: Some combos that end in BL~K,K(BE) can be TECHED to the right to escape by some characters. Those combos have been marked with double asterisks (**).

Some notations to know:

WS = "While Standing" aka, while your character is rising from a crouch position.
WR = While Running
BT = Back Turned
RO = Right Outer stance
RC = Right Cross Stance
LO = Left Outer stance
LI = Left Inner Stance
BL = Behind Lower Stance
PSL1 = Holding 6 briefly while in a stance to advance while switching stance
PSL2 = Holding 4 briefly while in a stance to retreat while switching stance
