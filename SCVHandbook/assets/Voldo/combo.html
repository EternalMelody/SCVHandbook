Alright, I'm listing this combo separately because the last hit can be teched out of to the left.

1K w! 1K w! 666B {B+K} MCHT K --- 76 damage if they lay there and take the last K or if they forward tech. 66 damage if they tech left out of the last hit, but if they tech right or back it's 96-98 damage

All wall combos that end with 2K can also be ended with BS 8WR B for virtually identical damage, but 2K is harder to mess up. You might consider BS 8WR B for wake up options, because BS 8WRB ends with Voldo in front turned, and BS 2K leaves him in back turn
