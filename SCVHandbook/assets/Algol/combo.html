Character Specific:
- 4B+K > 2A+B > 66B+K (103) works on Lex, Pyr/O Pyr, Pat (not alpha), and elysium. it can work on Xiba but seems very picky
- CH 1B > 2A+B > 66B+K (78) works on Pyrrha up close
- 22B > 2A+B can be left ukemi by Pyrrha and similar chars. asta/nm/sieg will get hit if you're close enough though, allowing for a 66B/66B BE
- during a 3B BE full meter combo the 2nd QI B in the combo will whiff on asta
vs Ezio, Raphael, Viola, Hilde, Cervy, Maxi, Edgemaster do:
3B BE > QI B > 3B BE > QI A+BB BE > delay > 3B BE QI B
it's not too hard to get if you pay attention to their height or just get used to the delay timing needed. delay too long and you wont get a groundsplat

- 4BA is prone to whiffing in juggles against asta/nm if they back right air control. use 1K as a replacement after the 66B BE for a guaranteed alternative
- 3B (w!) > 6KK (w!) > 4BA > QI A+BB (91, work on Asta and NM but 4BA whiff on pyrrha. depending on the angle this may whiff on asta as well)
- 3B (w!) > 6KK (w!) > 66B > 3B (75/85 if you can get 3BB. work on asta not pyrrha or NM)
- running low K > 3B (vs pyrrha)
- running low K > 3BB (vs aeon/cervy(?)/nightmare/sieg/yoshi(?) midscreen)
- running low K > BT 2B (vs asta)
- running low K > 1K (vs algol, really weird one, can't get 3B but can get 1K?)

***these combos leave you with 25% meter but don't allow you to use it to continue the combo further

Other Notes:
- For combo ender purposes, 66B gives more meter while FC 8B does more damage
- 623B is disadvantage on ground hit. 22B and 44B are advantage on hit alternatives that give slightly less meter
- running low K combos are weird and need more testing
- damage on combos with FC 8B BE > 44B still needs more testing. the 44B gets scaled a lot and I haven't confirmed that it can do full damage in match play, it may be a training mode issue
